About Unation FC

Unations FC, based in Worcester, MA, was founded in 2000 and became actively competitive in 2004. Since its inception, the club has established itself as a beacon of semi-professional soccer excellence, competing across multiple prestigious leagues and tournaments. With a robust structure comprising two teams, the D1 and premier teams, Unations FC offers a platform for aspiring players to showcase their skills and elevate their game to new heights.

The club's competitive journey spans prestigious competitions including the Twilight Summer League, Boston Soccer League (BSSL), American Soccer League (ASL), and currently the United Premier Soccer League (UPSL). A crowning achievement was participating in the US Open Cup, demonstrating our ability to compete at the highest levels of American soccer while helping elevate the standard of the sport in Worcester and beyond.

Within our framework, players have clear pathways to progress and excel. Those who demonstrate exceptional performance and commitment can advance from the D1 team to the premier team, where competition intensifies and stakes rise. This structure rewards talent while reinforcing our commitment to player development, making us a vital stepping stone for athletes pursuing professional soccer careers.

More than just a soccer team, Unations FC embodies two decades of growth, competition, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. Through our rich history, unwavering commitment to development, competitive success, and deep community engagement, we continue to leave an indelible mark on the soccer landscape, both locally and nationally, while nurturing the next generation of soccer talent.
